School Scholarships - Eligibility:

Each school scholarship comes with certain pre-defined set of eligibility conditions. These conditions are generally based on students' academic qualification and financial conditions. At times, some scholarship are also restricted to specific geographical locations. To get a detailed idea of the eligibility criteria for all school scholarships, go through the table given below. It provides you with in-depth information on scholarship eligibility.
